Free Shipping Policy

At RIOS, we strive to provide you with the best shopping experience possible. As part of this commitment, we offer free shipping on qualifying orders to help you save money while enjoying our premium products.

Free Shipping Eligibility

All orders with a total value of ₹999 or more qualify for FREE standard shipping across India.

Standard Shipping (Free for orders ₹999+)

  • Delivery Time: 4-7 business days for most locations in India
  • Tracking: Full tracking available through your account dashboard
  • Coverage: Available for 18,000+ PIN codes across India
  • Handling Time: Orders are typically processed within 24-48 hours

For orders below ₹999, a standard shipping charge of ₹79 will be applied at checkout.

Terms & Conditions

Exclusions from Free Shipping

Certain products are excluded from our free shipping offer due to their size, weight, or special handling requirements:

  • Oversized items (furniture, large appliances)
  • Items requiring special handling or refrigeration
  • Hazardous materials
  • Products shipped directly from third-party sellers

Delivery Areas

We deliver to most locations across India. However, some remote areas may experience longer delivery times or additional shipping charges. You can check if your location is serviceable during checkout.

Order Tracking

All shipments include tracking information that will be shared via email and SMS once your order is dispatched. You can also track your order in real-time through your account dashboard.

Shipping Delays

While we strive to meet our delivery estimates, delays may occur due to:

  • Adverse weather conditions
  • Natural disasters
  • Holidays and peak seasons
  • Customs clearance (for international orders)
  • Incorrect delivery information provided

Returns and Exchanges

If you need to return or exchange an item, please refer to our Return Policy for detailed instructions. Free return shipping is provided for most items within 15 days of delivery.

Frequently Asked Questions

When will my order ship?

Orders are typically processed within 24-48 hours of payment confirmation. You will receive a confirmation email with tracking information once your order ships.

How do I track my order?

You can track your order by logging into your account and navigating to "Order History" or by using the tracking link sent to your registered email and phone number.

Do you ship to PO boxes?

Yes, we ship to PO boxes for standard shipping options. However, express shipping services may require a physical street address.

What if I'm not available to receive my delivery?

Our delivery partners will attempt delivery up to 3 times. If you're unavailable, they will leave a delivery attempt notification with instructions for rescheduling or picking up your package.

Are there any hidden charges?

No. All applicable shipping charges are transparently displayed at checkout before you complete your purchase. For international orders, please note that customs duties and taxes are not included in our shipping fees and may be collected upon delivery.
