Frequently Asked Questions

How do I apply a promo code?
To apply a promo code, add items to your cart and proceed to checkout. On the checkout page, you'll find a field labeled "Promo Code" or "Discount Code." Enter your code in this field and click "Apply" to see the discount reflected in your total.
Can I use multiple promo codes on one order?
No, you can only use one promo code per order. If you have multiple codes, choose the one that offers the best value for your specific purchase. Some promotional offers may be automatically applied without a code if they provide better savings.
What should I do if my promo code isn't working?
If your promo code isn't working, check the following: 1) Verify that the code is entered correctly with no extra spaces. 2) Check if the code has expired. 3) Ensure your purchase meets any minimum requirements. 4) Verify that the items in your cart are eligible for the promotion. If you still have issues, contact our customer support for assistance.
Are there any restrictions on using promo codes?
Yes, promo codes may have restrictions such as: minimum purchase amounts, specific product categories, exclusion of certain brands, usage limits per customer, and expiration dates. Always check the terms and conditions associated with each promo code for specific restrictions.
How often are new promo codes released?
We release new promo codes regularly throughout the year. Special promotions are offered during holidays, seasonal changes, and special events. To stay updated on our latest promo codes, subscribe to our newsletter, follow us on social media, or regularly check this page.
